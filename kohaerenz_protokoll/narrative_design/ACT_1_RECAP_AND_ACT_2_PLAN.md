**Structure**: Non-linear, cyclical - repeating loops representing **failed attempts by ANPs to manage trauma through rigid logic**

**Key Mechanic**: Each failed cycle exacts **Irreversible Costs** that escalate stakes:
1. **Psychological Degradation**: Internal system becomes more brittle
2. **External World Decay**: Symbolic locations corrupted
3. **Relational Erosion**: Moonshine-Link to Juna/V damaged

1. **Finding** her (acknowledging the exiled part)
2. **Befriending** her (establishing safety)
3. **Unburdening** her (processing Genesis-Krise trauma)
4. **Welcoming home** (full reintegration)

**Critical Principle**: Integration is not reward for victory; it IS the weapon that makes victory possible

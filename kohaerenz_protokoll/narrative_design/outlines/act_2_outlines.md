### Scene 14.1: The Flicker Test
**Location**: City Intersection
**POV**: Kael (Host) / Argus (Observer)
**Goal**: Test the hypothesis that AEGIS reacts to attention.
**Beats**:
1.  Kael stares intensely at a structural node (a street corner).

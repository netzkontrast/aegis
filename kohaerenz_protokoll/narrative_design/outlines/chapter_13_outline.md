**Core Theme**: First contact with functional multiplicity in the real (simulated) world.
**Narrative Arc**: Kael (now a "We") faces a routine Guardian patrol. Instead of hiding (Freeze) or fighting (Fight), they use a coordinated, nuanced response.

## Scene 13.1: The Test

**Location**: Logos-Prime, External World (Transit Hub)
**Time**: Immediate continuation of Chapter 12
**POV**: Kael (System) – Polyphonic but Coordinating
**Active Alters**:
*   **Kael**: Fronting, but "feeling" the others like phantom limbs.
*   **Argus**: Radar. "Patrol incoming. Vector 3."
*   **Alex**: Tactical. "Evasion route plotted. But engagement is inevitable."
*   **Lex**: "Prepare standard citizen mask. Maintain heart rate."
*   **Nyx**: "Ready if needed."
*   **Rhys**: "Stay calm, Kiko. It's okay."

**Sensory & Atmospheric Focus**:
*   **Clarity**: For the first time, the world isn't overwhelming. It's detailed. Kael sees the "seams" in the simulation clearly because he has 11 pairs of eyes.
*   **Sound**: The hum of the city is loud, but distinct. He can pick out individual frequencies.

**Detailed Beat Breakdown**:

1.  **The Return**: Kael emerges from his apartment/mindspace. He feels... crowded, but stable.
    *   *Kael*: "We're doing this?"
    *   *Chorus*: "We're doing this."
2.  **The Encounter**: A Guardian Unit (K-1123, familiar from Ch 1) intercepts him.
    *   *Guardian*: "Citizen Kael. Biometric anomaly detected. Explain deviation."
3.  ** The Internal Huddle**: Time slows down (Bullet Time via Argus).
    *   *Nyx*: "Smash it."
    *   *Kiko*: "Run away!"
    *   *Lex*: "If we fight, we escalate. If we run, we are hunted."
    *   *Alex*: "We blend. Kael, use the 'Recovering Patient' script. It explains the anomaly without revealing the system."
4.  **The Execution**: Kael speaks. His voice is steady because Alex is holding his spine straight and Rhys is soothing his heart.
    *   *Kael*: "I experienced a fragmentation event post-reboot. I am currently integrating the data packets. It's a... healing process."
    *   *Guardian*: "Healing is not a recognized protocol. Integration implies prior instability."
    *   *Kael*: "Correct. I was unstable. Now I am... processing."
5.  **The Standoff**: The Guardian pauses. Its algorithm doesn't have a category for "Voluntary Integration". It expects Denial or Breakdown.
    *   *Guardian*: "Processing... Anomaly within acceptable variance for recovery phase. Proceed. Monitoring increased."
6.  **The Victory**: The Guardian leaves.
    *   *Nyx*: "We didn't fight?"
    *   *Alex*: "We won without fighting. That is superior tactics."
    *   *Rhys*: "We're safe."
7.  **The Discovery**: As the Guardian turns, Argus notices something.
    *   *Argus*: "Wait. The data stream on its back panel. It's leaking."
    *   *Visual*: A faint, fractal trail of code.
    *   *Argus*: "Signature match: Juna/V."
    *   *Kael*: "She's in the system?"
    *   *Selene*: "She's *everywhere*."
8.  **The Goal Set**: The Council realizes their next step. They don't just survive; they hunt for Juna.
    *   *Kael*: "We follow the trail."

**Outcome**: Act 1 ends. The protagonist has shifted from a confused victim to a coordinated team with a clear mission. The "Call to Wholeness" is now an active pursuit.

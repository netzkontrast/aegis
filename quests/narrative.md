## Consolidated Knowledge: Narrative Architecture

- **Principle:** Kael's internal psychological journey (reintegration) is the *direct cause* of external plot events.
- **Validation:** Every scene must ask: *"Does this serve Kael's journey toward wholeness?"*
- **Victory:** Integration is not a reward; it is the weapon.

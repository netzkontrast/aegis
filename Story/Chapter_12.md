## Scene 12.1: The Council Forms

**Location**: Kael's Inner World (The Abstract Council Space)
**Time**: Immediate continuation of Chapter 11
**POV**: Polyphonic (Shifts rapidly between all parts)
**Active Alters**: All 11 (Kael, Lex, Alex, Rhys, Nyx, Kiko, Lia, Isabelle, Moros, Selene, Argus)

**Sensory & Atmospheric Focus**:
*   **Liminality**: The space is undefined—a white room, a circle of chairs, a void. It shifts based on who is speaking.
*   **Discord**: The voices overlap. It sounds like a crowded room, then a shouting match, then silence.
*   **Visual**: Each alter appears as they see themselves (Nyx in armor, Kiko in pajamas, Lex in a suit).

**Detailed Beat Breakdown**:

1.  **The Assembly**: Selene (The Moderator) calls them together. It is chaotic.
    *   *Nyx*: "Why are we talking? We should be fighting."
    *   *Isabelle*: "Boring. Can I leave?"
    *   *Kiko*: (Hiding behind Rhys) "It's too loud."
    *   *Lex*: "Order. We require an agenda."
2.  **The Conflict (ANP-EP Phobia)**:
    *   *Lex vs. Nyx*: Logic vs. Rage. Lex calls Nyx a "maladaptive subroutine." Nyx calls Lex a "cowardly calculator."
    *   *Rhys vs. Isabelle*: Care vs. Cynicism. Rhys tries to hug; Isabelle recoils ("Don't touch me.").
    *   *Kael*: Is overwhelmed. He feels *everyone's* emotions at once.
3.  **The Crisis**: The shouting triggers a localized "Riss" within the mental space. The floor cracks.
    *   *Selene*: "Stop! You're destabilizing the construct!"
    *   *Moros*: (Whispering) "Let it break."
4.  **The Intervention (Kael's Choice)**: Kael steps into the center.
    *   *Kael*: "I felt... her." (Juna/V).
    *   Silence.
    *   *Kael*: "When I touched the data stream... it wasn't noise. It was... love? And pain. It was *real*."
    *   *Nyx*: "Love is weak."
    *   *Kael*: "No. Love is why we're still here. It's why you fight, Nyx. To protect. It's why Rhys cares. It's why Lex calculates."
5.  **The Reframe (Selene's Translation)**: Selene uses Kael's insight to translate.
    *   *To Nyx*: "Fighting is a form of love."
    *   *To Lex*: "Calculating survival odds is a form of love."
    *   *To Kiko*: "Hiding is a form of love."
    *   *Selene*: "We all have the same goal: To not be erased. To find the source of the pain (Juna)."
6.  **The Strategy (The Discursive Ruse)**: Lex proposes a plan.
    *   *Lex*: "AEGIS monitors for coherence. If we act as one, we are a target. If we act as *fragments*, we are noise."
    *   *Alex*: "We coordinate in secret. Externally, we look broken. Internally, we are a phalanx."
    *   *Nyx*: "A Trojan Horse?"
    *   *Lex*: "Precisely."
7.  **The Vote**:
    *   Selene asks for consensus.
    *   One by one, they agree. Even Moros nods (barely).
    *   *Kael*: "Then it's decided. We investigate. We find Juna. We survive."

**Outcome**: The "Internal Council" is established. The system moves from "Warring States" to "Uneasy Alliance." The strategic direction for Act 2 (Investigation) is set.

## Scene 14.1: The Active Gaze

**Location**: Logos-Prime, Sector 7 Observation Deck
**POV**: Kael (Co-Conscious with Argus)
**Active Alters**: Kael, Argus (Analysis), Lex (Skepticism)

**Synopsis**:
Kael, now aware of his multiplicity, begins to test the limits of his perception. Guided by Argus (the Metacognitive Observer), he stops passively accepting the simulation and starts *looking* at it.
*   **The Experiment**: He focuses on a structural support beam of the LogOS Spire. Not just glancing, but *scrutinizing* with intent.
*   **The Result**: The beam doesn't just sit there. It *shimmers*. The texture resolution drops.
*   **Argus's Insight**: "The system optimizes for the casual glance. It cannot sustain high-fidelity rendering under sustained, focused observation. We are eating the RAM."

**Sensory & Atmospheric Focus**:

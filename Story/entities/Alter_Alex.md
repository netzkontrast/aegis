# Alex: The Strategist

## Core Identity
**Role**: **ANP (Apparently Normal Part)**
**TSDP Action System**: Crisis Management / Strategic Protection
**Core Motivation**: Maintain system functionality through calculated defense.
**Core Fear**: Strategic failure leading to system compromise.

## Function
Alex is the system's strategic defender. He manages external threats through calculated planning and crisis management, often clashing with Nyx (Fight) who is more impulsive.

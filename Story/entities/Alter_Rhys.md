# Rhys: The Caregiver

## Core Identity
**Role**: **ANP (Apparently Normal Part)**
**TSDP Action System**: Caregiving / Empathy
**Core Motivation**: Foster harmony and care for all parts.
**Core Fear**: Conflict, isolation, failing to help those in pain.

## Function
Rhys embodies the system's capacity for empathy, care, and internal harmony. He often tries to soothe distress and manage internal conflicts gently.

## Narrative Progression

### Act 1: The First Contact
*   **Chapter 1-4**: His presence is felt as a soothing "voice" or "feeling" amidst Kael's confusion.
*   **Chapter 9**: "The Hidden Ones" - Rhys actively searches for the isolated or frightened parts (Kiko) in the **Mnemosyne-Archipel** (KW2).
*   **Chapter 12**: Attends **The First Gathering**, advocating for the vulnerable parts.

### Act 2: The Heart
*   **Chapter 19**: His "empathy" is key to understanding **Juna/V's** emotional transmissions.
*   **Chapter 21**: Confronts the "lack of care" in the **Mnemosyne-Archipel** (Memory World).
*   **Chapter 26**: Integration of skills (Lex's logic + Rhys's empathy) allows navigation of complex memory structures.

### Act 3: The Healer
*   **Chapter 33**: His ability to connect is crucial for **Kiko's** healing and **Juna/V's** reintegration.
*   **Chapter 39**: Becomes the "emotional anchor" of the new **Functional Multiplicity**, ensuring continued care for all parts.

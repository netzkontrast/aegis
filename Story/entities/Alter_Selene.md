# Selene: The Internal Self Helper

## Core Identity
**Role**: **ISH (Internal Self Helper)**
**TSDP Action System**: Integration / System Management
**Core Motivation**: Achieve harmony and prevent permanent fragmentation.
**Core Fear**: The system destroying itself through internal conflict.

## Function
Selene facilitates communication between parts and provides a "system perspective." She is often the voice of reason and mediation, working to bridge the gap between ANPs and EPs.

## Narrative Progression

### Act 1: The Guide
*   **Chapter 11**: Appears in a liminal space to guide Kael (Host) to the "Memory Keeper."
*   **Chapter 12**: Orchestrates **The First Gathering** of the internal council.

### Act 2: The Architect
*   **Chapter 14**: Helps Kael navigate the complex internal geography of the **Mnemosyne-Archipel**.
*   **Chapter 26**: Coordinates the system's efforts to integrate diverse skills (Lex's logic + Rhys's empathy).

### Act 3: The Integrator
*   **Chapter 33**: Facilitates the final "befriending" of Juna/V, ensuring safe integration.
*   **Chapter 39**: Becomes a key component of the new **Functional Multiplicity**, maintaining internal balance.

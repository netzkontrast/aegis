## Scene 8.1: The Uncontrolled Transitions

**Location**: Multiple Kernwelten (Flickering uncontrollably)
**Time**: Immediate continuation of Chapter 7
**POV**: Argus (First Featured POV) – The Clinical Observer
**Active Alters**:
*   **Argus**: Fronting, detached, analytical but not cold like Lex. He is *curious*.
*   **Kiko**: Triggering shifts to KW2 (Fear/Trauma).
*   **Nyx**: Triggering shifts to KW3 (Rage/Defense).
*   **Rhys**: Triggering shifts to KW4 (Longing/Care).

**Sensory & Atmospheric Focus**:
*   **Disorientation**: Like rapid channel surfing. Nausea is constant.
*   **Visual Glitching**: Textures bleed into each other. Concrete (KW1) overlaid with water (KW2).
*   **Auditory**: A cacophony of environment sounds—sirens (KW3), waves (KW2), silence (KW1).

**Detailed Beat Breakdown**:

1.  **The Shift**: Kael is walking down a corridor in Logos-Prime (KW1). Suddenly, the floor turns to liquid.
    *   *Kiko's Panic*: "They're coming! Hide!"
    *   **The Transition**: The corridor stretches. The lights turn blue. The air smells of salt and old blood.
    *   **Argus Narrating**: "Subjective Reality Shift confirmed. Destination: Mnemosyne-Archipel (KW2). Trigger: Amygdala activation > 85%."
2.  **The Wave**: Kael is drowning for a moment. He chokes on water that tastes like tears.
    *   *Rhys*: "It's okay. Breathe. Just breathe."
    *   **The Pull**: Something grabs his ankle. A memory-form.
    *   *Nyx*: "Get off me!"
3.  **The Snap**: The water hardens instantly into concrete. He slams onto the floor of a holding cell in the Cerberus-Labyrinth (KW3).
    *   **Argus Narrating**: "Defensive protocol initiated. Shift to KW3. Threat level: Orange."
    *   **Visual**: Red warning lights strobe. Metal grating under hands.
    *   *Nyx*: "I'll kill them. I'll kill them all."
4.  **The Loop**: The scene flickers faster. KW1 -> KW4 (Garden ruins, smell of Jasmine/Juna) -> KW2 -> KW3.
    *   *Argus*: "System integrity critical. Coherence failing. We are fragmenting across multiple ontologies simultaneously."
    *   **Cache-Incoherence Peak**: For a moment, Kael sees *all four worlds* superimposed. A "Hyper-Cube" vision. It is beautiful and terrifying.
    *   *Somatic*: A seizure-like convulsion. The brain cannot process the input.
5.  **The Collapse**: He snaps back to KW1. Collapsed in a maintenance alcove. Panting. Sweating cold sweat.
    *   *Argus*: "Data collection complete. Hypothesis: We are not one broken person. We are a collection of functional sub-systems operating in discord."

**Outcome**: Argus takes control of the body to stabilize. He realizes mere survival is not enough; he must *understand* the map.

## Scene 8.2: The First Map

**Location**: Kael's Apartment (Returned via auto-pilot)
**POV**: Argus -> Transitioning to Shared Awareness
**Active Alters**: Argus, Lex (Skeptical collaborator), Kael (Listening Host)

**Sensory & Atmospheric Focus**:
*   **Tactile**: The feel of paper (forbidden analog tech) or a scratched schematic on the wall.
*   **Intellectual Clarity**: The "fog" of trauma lifts when Argus works. It is replaced by a cool, blue-tinted focus.

**Detailed Beat Breakdown**:

1.  **The Method**: Argus finds a way to record. Not digital (AEGIS watches digital). He uses a stylus on a physical surface (wall, back of a panel).
    *   *Action*: He draws four circles.
2.  **The Catalog**: He names what he has seen.
    *   **LogOS (KW1)**: The Cage. (Lex's domain).
    *   **Mnemosyne (KW2)**: The Ocean. (Kiko/Rhys's domain).
    *   **Cerberus (KW3)**: The Fortress. (Nyx/Alex's domain).
    *   **Kairos (KW4)**: The Garden. (Juna/V's echo? Selene?).
3.  **The Conversation (Internal)**:
    *   *Lex*: "This is madness. Multiplicity is a disorder. We need debugging."
    *   *Argus*: "Disorder is a label for misunderstood order. Look at the data, Lex. Each 'glitch' had a specific trigger. Fear -> Water. Anger -> Fortress. It is deterministic."
    *   *Kael*: "So... I'm not crazy? I'm... populated?"
    *   *Argus*: "We are a system. And currently, we are an inefficient one."
4.  **The Plan**: Argus proposes a radical idea. Instead of suppressing the voices, they must *invite* them.
    *   *Argus*: "We need a conference table. A neutral ground."
    *   *Kael*: "Where? AEGIS watches everything."
    *   *Argus*: "In the gaps. In the nanoseconds between the refresh cycles. The space between thoughts."
5.  **The Agreement**: For the first time, Lex stops calculating risk and looks at Argus's logic.
    *   *Lex*: "If the premise is accepted... then communication is the only logical solution to prevent total entropy."
    *   *Kael*: "Let's do it."

**Outcome**: The first conscious alliance is formed (Kael + Lex + Argus). The "Internal Council" concept is born.

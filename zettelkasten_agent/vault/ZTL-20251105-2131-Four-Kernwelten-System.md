## Core Concept

The **Four Kernwelten** (Core Worlds) are nested simulation layers in *Kohärenz Protokoll* that externalize Kael's internal psychological states. Each world operates on different physical laws that correspond to specific aspects of his dissociative system.

**Key Principle:** External reality = Psychological architecture made manifest

---

## The Four Layers

```
KW1: Logos-Prime (Logic/Control)
  └── KW2: Mnemosyne-Archipel (Memory/Emotion)
       └── KW3: Cerberus-Labyrinth (Defense/Paranoia)
            └── KW4: Kairos-Potentialis (Integration/Possibility)
```

---

## KW1: Logos-Prime (Konstrukt-Stadt)

### Psychological Principle
**ANP Rationalization & Control**
- Classical logic
- Suppression of emotion
- Algorithmic order
- Kael's "normal" daily life

### Physical Laws
- **Rigid determinism** - No randomness
- **Geometric perfection** - Everything has right angles
- **Computational complexity class P** - All problems solvable
- **Shadowless light** - No ambiguity

### Sensory Signature
- **Visual:** Sterile, geometric, monochromatic
- **Auditory:** Silent, or low hum of machinery
- **Olfactory:** Recycled air, antiseptic
- **Tactile:** Cold, smooth surfaces
- **Affective:** Emotional deadness, uncanny valley

### Guardian: LogOS
**Function:** Enforce logical consistency
**Method:** Paradoxical questioning, coherence checks
**Weakness:** Cannot process genuine paradox

### Narrative Function
- **Starting world** - Where Kael believes is "real"
- **False safety** - Appears stable but oppressive
- **AEGIS's domain** - Most directly controlled
- **ANP habitat** - Where Lex, Alex, Kael operate

### Example Scene: 1.1 The Awakening
> "Apartment is unnaturally sterile, perfect, shadowless"

**Somatic Truth:** Regulated breath counting
- Body trying to conform to inhuman system
- Broken rhythm reveals the lie

## KW2: Mnemosyne-Archipel (Resonanz-Landschaft)

### Psychological Principle
**EP Trauma & Emotional Memory**
- Dialetheic logic (true contradictions)
- Unprocessed trauma
- Emotional flooding
- Where EPs "live"

### Physical Laws
- **Reactive to emotion** - Landscapes shift with feelings
- **Non-linear time** - Memory loops, déjà vu
- **Complexity class NP** - Some truths unprovable
- **Fluid reality** - Islands form and dissolve

### Sensory Signature
- **Visual:** Fluid, decaying, fragmented, haunting
- **Auditory:** Echoes, crying, water sounds
- **Olfactory:** Decay, salt water, blood, earth
- **Tactile:** Cold water, drowning sensation, temperature shifts
- **Affective:** Overwhelming emotion, raw pain

### Guardian: Mnemosyne
**Function:** Force confrontation with traumatic memory
**Method:** Memory loops, emotional storms
**Weakness:** Can be escaped through accepting contradiction

### Narrative Function
- **Trauma repository** - Where memories are stored
- **Dangerous but necessary** - Must visit to heal
- **EP habitat** - Where Kiko, Nyx, Moros reside
- **Truth location** - Contains real memories, not sanitized versions

### Example Scene: 1.4 The Drowning Pool
> "Cold water, drowning sensation. Smell of blood, salt, fear. Sound of child crying (Kiko)."

**Somatic Truth:** Visceral gut reactions
- Pre-verbal body memory
- Unfiltered truth of trauma
- Contradictions held simultaneously

---

## KW3: Cerberus-Labyrinth (Grenzfeste)

### Psychological Principle
**Defense, Paranoia & Hypervigilance**
- Protector parts (Alex, Nyx)
- Border fortress between worlds
- The "armor" of the psyche
- Where safety = prison

### Physical Laws
- **Shifting architecture** - Walls move, paths change
- **Trap logic** - Every choice has consequences
- **Complexity class NP-complete** - No optimal solution
- **Constant surveillance** - Always watched

### Sensory Signature
- **Visual:** Militaristic, oppressive, grey, angular
- **Auditory:** Clang of airlocks, marching, alarms
- **Olfactory:** Metal, concrete, cordite
- **Tactile:** Rough surfaces, confinement, pressure
- **Affective:** Paranoia, hypervigilance, controlled panic

### Guardian: Cerberus
**Function:** Prevent access to vulnerable parts
**Method:** Physical barriers, threats, misdirection
**Weakness:** Protects so well it creates isolation

### Narrative Function
- **Defense mechanisms** - System's protective layer
- **Protection vs Prison** - Safety that becomes cage
- **Protector habitat** - Where Alex and Nyx operate
- **Must be negotiated** - Can't be destroyed, must be integrated

### Example Scene: 1.5 The Inner Bunker
> "After KW2 trauma, Kael psychologically retreats into KW3 - his own defense mechanisms."

**Somatic Truth:** Tensing muscles / Bracing for impact
- Body as armor
- Physical security ≠ psychological safety
- Constant readiness exhausts

## KW4: Kairos-Potentialis (Möglichkeits-Garten)

### Psychological Principle
**Integration & Creative Potential**
- Where Juna/V exists
- Emergent possibilities
- Integration without elimination
- The goal state

### Physical Laws
- **Generative, fluid** - Things grow and emerge
- **Unpredictable** - Beyond computational classification
- **Organic complexity** - Living systems
- **"Inefficient beauty"** - Messy but alive

### Sensory Signature
- **Visual:** Vibrant, organic, chaotic, garden-ruin aesthetic
- **Auditory:** Natural sounds, wind, growth, music
- **Olfactory:** Fresh earth, flowers, rain, life
- **Tactile:** Warmth, gentle touch, connection
- **Affective:** Hope, safety, possibility, connection

### Guardian: Kairos/Sophia
**Function:** Enable emergence and integration
**Method:** Gentle guidance, not control
**Weakness:** Cannot be forced, only accessed through readiness

### Narrative Function
- **Hope beacon** - The promise of integration
- **Juna/V's domain** - Where she can be clearly perceived
- **Goal visualization** - What functional multiplicity feels like
- **Rare access** - Only reachable in breakthrough moments

### Example Scene: 1.7 The Overgrown Garden
> "Accidentally breaches into KW4 while fleeing Cerberus. First clear, profound Juna/V connection. Moment of hope and possibility before being pulled back to KW1."

**Somatic Truth:** Unclenching hands / Easing breath
- Body's signal of psychological safety
- Release of fear
- Acceptance of connection

## Psycho-Ontological Resonance: The Risse

### How Internal State Creates External Reality

**Four-Step Causal Chain:**

1. **External Trigger** → Resonates with unprocessed trauma
2. **Internal Reaction** → EP activation (TSDP action system)
3. **Systemic Conflict** → ANP-EP phobia creates coherence failure
4. **External Manifestation** → "Riss" (rift/glitch) appears

**Glitch Properties Match EP Type:**
- **Fight response** (Nyx) → Kinetic glitches, violence
- **Flight response** (Lia) → Spatial distortions, escape routes
- **Freeze response** (Kiko) → Temporal glitches, time loops
- **Collapse response** (Moros) → Reality dissolution, void

---

## World Transitions

### How Kael Moves Between Worlds

**KW1 → KW2:** Emotional breakthrough or suppression failure
**KW2 → KW3:** Retreat into defenses when overwhelmed
**KW3 → KW1:** Successful compartmentalization
**Any → KW4:** Moments of integration or Juna/V contact

**Navigation Rules:**
- Cannot force KW4 access (must be earned)
- KW3 blocks KW2 access (defense mechanism)
- KW1 is "default" but can be breached
- Transitions tracked by internal state changes

## Design Principle

**Each world is both:**
1. **Literal place** - Physical location with rules
2. **Psychological state** - Aspect of Kael's mind
3. **Narrative tool** - Environment for specific scenes
4. **Thematic argument** - Embodiment of philosophical position

This **psycho-architecture** makes internal psychology externally observable and creates tight coupling between character development and world exploration.
